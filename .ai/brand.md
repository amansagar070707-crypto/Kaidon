# Kaidon Brand Rules

## Identity

- Brand name: Kaidon.
- Category: open-source Agent Operating System.
- Product lines: Runtime, SDK, CLI, Studio, Cloud.
- Voice: serious infrastructure, not a chatbot wrapper.

## Logo

- Use the approved monoline geometric mark only.
- Keep the open hexagon with the escaping diagonal stroke intact.
- Do not add fills, gradients, shadows, bevels, or 3D effects to the mark.
- Use amber on dark backgrounds and near-black on light backgrounds only.

## Colors

- Base: `#0E1116`
- Panel: `#161A21`
- Border: `#262B35`
- Text: `#ECEEF2`
- Muted text: `#8B93A3`
- Signal accent: `#F5A623`
- Secondary: `#6E7BFF`

## Typography

- Use a geometric sans for display and body text.
- Use a monospace family for code, CLI, and data.
- Keep wordmarks bold with tight tracking.

## Visual Tone

- Calm, restrained, and infrastructure-grade.
- Soft geometry, subtle curves, thin borders, low-elevation depth.
- No purple AI gradients, no terracotta/cream branding, no neon-green accents.

## Copy Tone

- Use concise, technical, confident language.
- Avoid playful chatbot language.
- Keep product naming aligned to Kaidon Runtime, SDK, CLI, Studio, and Cloud.
