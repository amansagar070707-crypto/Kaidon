# Go To Market

## Purpose

Define the developer adoption strategy.

## Initial Audience

- OSS developers
- infra-minded founders
- technical AI product teams
- internal platform teams

## Entry Wedge

“Make your agent observable and durable without rebuilding infrastructure.”

## Why This Wedge

It targets real pain, not generic curiosity.

## Channels

- GitHub
- technical docs
- runtime demos
- benchmark reports
- template examples

## Tradeoffs

- Slower mass adoption than a consumer-facing product.
- Better fit for long-term platform credibility.

## Alternatives

- no-code go-to-market first
- coding assistant positioning first

## Verified Facts

- The product directive consistently targets developers rather than no-code
  users.

## Assumptions

- Developer trust is more important than broad brand awareness at first.

## Speculative Ideas

- A public “agent runtime scorecard” could become a high-leverage content
  engine for Kaidon.
